How Android 14 Reacts
🔴 Tapjacking Filters Triggered In this mode, each window carries its own invisible resize borders and handles. When App A overlaps App B, the invisible overlays of App B extend over App A's tap area.

Android 14 detects touch-intercept sheets overlapping another app, flag-marks the touch as "unsafe" (tapjacking prevention), and **completely blocks or delays touches** inside that overlapping space.
